Breakfast

Ingredients

  • Plain Bagel
  • 1 Egg
  • Muenster Cheese
  • 1/4 Yellow Onion, sliced
  • Olive Oil
  • Great Value Seasoned Sirloin Philly Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt & Pepper
  • McCormick Smokehouse Maple Seasoning
  • Mustard
  • worcestershire

Instructions

  1. In a hot skillet add some olive oil and butter.
  2. Add the yellow onions in an effort to carmelize them.
  3. Once, the onions are translucent add the frozen Philly Steak.
  4. Season the frozen patty with salt, pepper, and McCormick Smokehouse Maple Seasoning.
  5. Once the patty defrost due to the heat flip over and season the other side.
  6. Turn the oven on the broil setting.
  7. Butter your bagel and place in oven to toast.
  8. Add a slice of muenster cheese on the bottom bagel to melt.
  9. In the skillet add a slice of muenster cheese on the sirloin steak and slide to one side.
  10. Crack an egg in the same skillet.
  11. Season with salt and Pepper.
  12. Flip over the egg in an effort to make it over easy.
  13. Now, place the bagel on a plate add mustard then add your meat,onions and egg and enjoy!

This is not the healthiest breakfast option. However, it is fatty and delicious which is the perfect combination after a hangover!

Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 celery stalk
  • 1/2 yellow Onion
  • 1/2 green bell pepper
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 pound crawfish tails
  • 2 tablespoons crushed garlic
  • 2 teaspoons flour
  • 2 1/2 cups half and half
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan
  • 1/2 cup shredded Romano cheese
  • 2 tablespoons Liquid crab boil
  • onion and garlic powder to taste
  • Cajun spices
  • 1 pound pasta your choice
  • chopped green onions garnish

Instructions

  1. Finely chop veggies
  2. Melt butter and saute veggies
  3. Add garlic
  4. Sprinkle in flour and spices
  5. Stir well until flour dissolves into the butter
  6. Add in half and half
  7. Simmer until it starts to thicken; do not bring to a boil
  8. Add in cheese
  9. Season crawfish with cajun spices and liquid crab boil
  10. Add crawfish to the sauce
  11. Taste and adjust seasoning
  12. Serve over pasta

I know pasta gets a bad wrap blah blah blah but Don't listen to the haters. After THIS recipe you will never want to use jar alfredo sauce ever again in life. I received so many compliments after I made this alfredo.

Dessert

Ingredients

  • 3-4 bananas
  • 3/4 cup of sugar
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 3 cups of milk
  • 3 tablespoons salted butter
  • splash of pure vanilla extract
  • 1 pkg cream cheese, softened
  • 2 tablespoons of lemon juice
  • 1-2 pkgs vanilla wafers
  • 1 tub of cool whip

Instructions

  1. Mix sugar, cornstarch, and egg yolks well
  2. Add to a heavy bottomed saucepan
  3. Whisk in milk slowly on medium heat
  4. Add butter
  5. Continue whisking and bring mixture to a simmer
  6. When it starts to thicken turn off the heat
  7. Add extract and cream cheese
  8. Whisk until smooth
  9. Allow to cool for 20 mins.
  10. Slice bananas and toss in lemon juice
  11. Layer in a dish or bowl starting with cookies and ending with cool whip
  12. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours

Now, I won't lie and say this dessert taste better than Big Momma's but it is second runner up.
